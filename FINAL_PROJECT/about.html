About Me

Hi! I am Angela Nicole Arrozal, a BSIT specializing in cybersecurity student at FEU Tech. Just a quick heads up about who I am…

I was born and raised here in Metro Manila, in Quezon City. I studied at NU Fairview for my SHS and pursued the STEM strand to broaden my knowledge in most aspects. My passion for IT only sparked after I graduated from high school and chose to pursue cybersecurity. In pursuing ongoing knowledge acquisition, I am continuing my education at FEU Tech.

In my professional journey as a student, I’ve been freelancing as a model in my free time and also had experience doing business during the pandemic. These experiences have given me a great deal of familiarity with and background on how the professional world works.

My work ethic has always been a key to my success. I flourish in the environment and can adjust to the opportunities and obstacles I am encountering. As I continue honing my skills and pursuing further education, I am eager to contribute my skills to my peers who value the same frame of mind as mine.

Thanks for getting to know me a bit better. I am excited about the potential and can't wait to showcase my relevant experience and portfolio projects with you.
